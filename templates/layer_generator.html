Understanding Attributes

Attributes are optional details that provide specific information about the object. They appear between the TYPE and PHASE in the layer name.

Size/Diameter:
12IN, 18IN, 24IN
Material:
PVC, HDPE, CONC, DI
Slope/Grade:
2PCT, 5PCT, 8PCT
Dimension:
5FT, 10FT, 500CF
Auto-extraction during export: When exporting from your database, attributes are automatically extracted from object properties (diameter, material, slope, etc.) - you don't need to manually specify them!
Fifth segment: Construction lifecycle (NEW, EXIST, DEMO, etc.)
Sixth segment: Geometry hint (LN, PT, PG, TX, etc.)
