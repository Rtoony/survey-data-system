About Standards Library

The Standards Library is your central repository for all reusable CAD standards. These standards can be:

  • Assigned to Projects: Link standards to specific projects for consistency
  • Reused Across Projects: Maintain consistency across all your engineering work
  • Updated Globally: Changes propagate to all projects using these standards
  • Exported and Shared: Share your standards with team members or other organizations
Best Practice: Establish your standards library first, then assign them to projects. This ensures consistency and makes future updates easier.
