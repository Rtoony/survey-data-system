Material Volume Calculator

Calculate material volumes for earthwork and construction quantity takeoffs

Volume & Earthwork Calculator (Prototype)

This specialized tool will calculate material volumes based on surface areas, material depths, and 3D elevation models to provide accurate quantity takeoffs for earthwork, base materials, pavement, and construction materials.

Planned Features

  • 3D surface-to-surface volume calculations (cut/fill)
  • Material depth assignment by layer/object type
  • Multi-layer material stacks (base, subbase, wearing surface)
  • Earthwork balance calculations
  • Unit conversion (CY, CF, tons based on material density)
  • Cost estimation integration
  • Export quantity takeoff reports
  • Integration with Area Calculator and grading models

Under Construction

This tool is currently in development. Check back soon for the full implementation.

Supported Volume Calculations

Earthwork

Cut/fill volumes, grading analysis, berms, slopes, pads

Pavement Sections

Base, subbase, asphalt, concrete quantities

BMP Storage

Detention volume, infiltration basin capacity

Material Takeoffs

Aggregate, soil, concrete, asphalt quantities

Calculation Method

Volume = Area (from Area Calculator) × Depth/Thickness (material property) × 3D Surface Model (elevation variations)

This tool will leverage data from the Area Calculator and integrate with your project's 3D grading models for accurate volume calculations.
