Pervious/Impervious Analysis

Analyze hardscape vs landscape for water absorption and runoff characteristics

Site Hydrology Analysis Tool (Prototype)

This specialized tool will analyze hardscape vs landscape features to determine water absorption rates, infiltration capacity, and runoff characteristics for comprehensive site hydrology assessment and stormwater management planning.

Planned Features

  • Automatic classification of pervious vs impervious surfaces
  • Infiltration rate assignment by surface type
  • Runoff coefficient calculations
  • Site coverage analysis (% impervious)
  • Water balance modeling
  • Visual heatmap of infiltration capacity
  • Compliance checking for local stormwater regulations
  • Integration with Area Calculator and BMP Manager

Under Construction

This tool is currently in development. Check back soon for the full implementation.

Surface Classification

Pervious Surfaces

  • Bioretention facilities
  • Rain gardens & bioswales
  • Detention basins
  • Infiltration basins
  • Vegetated swales
  • Landscape areas
  • Permeable paving (optional)

Impervious Surfaces

  • Accessible parking areas
  • Accessible paths & ramps
  • Building pads
  • Concrete surfaces
  • Asphalt surfaces
  • Plazas & hardscape
  • Rooftops
