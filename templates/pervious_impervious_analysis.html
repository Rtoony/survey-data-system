The Pervious/Impervious Analysis tool analyzes site surfaces to determine water absorption characteristics, infiltration capacity, and runoff generation. This tool classifies hardscape vs landscape features and calculates site hydrology metrics for stormwater management compliance.

This tool integrates with the BMP Manager and Area Calculator to provide comprehensive site hydrology assessment, helping ensure compliance with local stormwater regulations and supporting sustainable site design.
